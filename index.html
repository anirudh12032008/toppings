Welcome to ISRO

ISRO is a space agency which has its headquartered in Bengaluru, India

ISRO stands for Indian Space Research Organisation.

Mission

Chandrayaan 1

Chandrayaan 1 was India first mission to the Moon it was launched in 2008 ( I was born in that year lol )

Chandrayaan 2

Chandrayaan 2 was India second mission to the Moon it was launched in 2019

Mangalyaan

Mangalyaan was India first mission to Mars it was launched in 2013

Launch Vehicles

PSLV

PSLV (Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle) it is used to launch satellites into polar orbits

GSLV

GSLV (Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle) it is used to launch heavier payloads

SSLV

SSLV (Small Satellite Launch Vehicle) it is used to launch small satellites in low Earth orbit
